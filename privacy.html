Privacy Policy

Playvex is designed to work without accounts or logins. You can play games instantly without providing personal information.

When advertising is enabled in designated ad zones, third-party partners may use cookies or similar technologies to provide and measure ads. Playvex does not place ads during gameplay.

If analytics is enabled in the future, it will be used to understand overall performance and improve user experience, such as load times and device compatibility.

If you have questions about privacy on Playvex, you can add a contact address on the About page when you are ready to publish one.
