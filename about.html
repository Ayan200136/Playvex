About Playvex

Playvex is built for mobile-first instant play. You tap, the game loads, and you start. No installs, no accounts, and no distractions during gameplay.

Every game is delivered as a lightweight HTML5 experience designed for fast loading on mid-range Android devices and smooth, responsive controls in portrait orientation.

Playvex is designed to scale. New games are added through a simple JSON registry so the platform can grow without constant core-site changes.
